Project Background


The knowledge gap between the healthcare and technology industry has caused the gradual development of technology in the healthcare sector. It has been estimated that it takes around five years to develop software for the NHS. This is due to the continuous reviewing and communication required when developing a software as it needs to be checked by the NHS. Therefore, our PRIDAR Chatbot will help aid the development of softwares by allowing engineers to check that they have met the criteria for the NHS.
